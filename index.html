About us

Etisalat Foundation for Community Development & Care (EF) is an Egyptian non-profit organization launched under the Ministry of Social Solidarity in March 2013 and established under Law 84 of 2002. We are committed to helping shape and build communities, and improving livelihoods and aspirations for the future. EF aims to support the different Egyptian institutions to deliver proper healthcare services and help communities reach decent well-being standards. EF’s vision is “to be recognized as the best organization in Egypt contributing in the field of health development and well-being to attain global standards”. EF’s mission is “to capitalize on Etisalat Misr’s technical capabilities and resources to assist and provide the platforms necessary to improve the quality and efficiency of healthcare services in Egypt”.
